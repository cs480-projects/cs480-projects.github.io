A Planner Made for Simplicity

Our Concept

As college students, we often find that the world is on our shoulders; the real problem is, we can't find a simple way to schedule time to set it down. We sought out to solve that problem: to create a flexible, but simple way to check off your duties with an attractive, simple interface, and combine all of your apps into one. Everyone organizes differently, so with SmartPlanner, we want everyone to be able to find a way to plan with their strengths in mind.

Our Design Philosophy

Aesthetically, we chose Angular Material as our palette. It's simple, attractive and easy on the eyes. We felt that this adds to the simple and free feel of the app, and we wanted to adhere to that philosophy throughout the design. We also chose simple shapes in order to avoid distraction when utilizing the application. Choices and changes would be limited to the color of sticky notes and calendar dates. Designs for the background were talked about, but implementation is yet to be discussed.

Final Concept

Our final concept is a simple calendar, affixed with sticky notes for each date with a checklist attached. This is an extremely simple way to keep track of everything coming, and adheres to our idea of simplicity in flexibility. For future iterations, we plan to add notifications for the desktop, customization of application parts and more design customization and fixes. We are really excited about this project, and hopefully will continue to move forward with its creation!
