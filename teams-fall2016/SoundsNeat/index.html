Problem Overview

Have you ever been driving and wondered whether the area you're in is safe?

Have you ever gotten lost and wondered whether you were in a safe neighborhood?

Wouldn't it be great if a service could tell you the answer right away without trying to read all the data by yourself?

The solution is here!

The Am I Safe Here? web service is in development!!!

Features!!!

  1. Get your current location via HTML5 Geolocation and uses crime statistics to determine whether the city you are in is safe!!

  2. Improve the the city feature & Focus on the Neighborhood

  3. Globalize the feature to other countries...

  4. Navigation System with safest routes
