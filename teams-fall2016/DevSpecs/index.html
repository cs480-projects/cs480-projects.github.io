Despite all of the advances we have made in social media and communications technology, there are still places in our lives where it is easy for us to feel disconnected. We at DevSpecs believe that the classroom should not be one of those places. Another belief of ours is that video games have the power to connect people in a fun and productive way. Our primary mission is to take advantage of the pervasiveness of video games, and use it to build stronger communities in schools.
