What We Are Building:
Rate My Clubs

It's like Rate My Professors, but instead of professor ratings there are Cal Poly Pomona club ratings.

Anonymous Rating System

To respect student privacy, users do not have to associate their real names or usernames when rating clubs.

Clubs Rated on Multiple Scales

Users can rate clubs on multiple scales such as career-oriented, social, etc.

User Club Preferences and Querying

Users will be able to personalize their club preferences to align with what clubs they want to view.
Users can also query for clubs (most ratings, highest rated, etc).
