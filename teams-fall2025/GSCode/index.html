MyBarRaiser
by GSCode

A software engineering project for CS4800 Fall 2025

A newly reimagined version of Cal Poly Pomona's MyBar website, MyBarRaiser is designed with students in mind to find their connection to campus outside the classroom.

  • Current MyBar

New and Improved

Advanced Filtering

Tag-based club and organization searching with more advanced filters. Current filtering is simple word-matching that would only work if a student already knows what to search for.

Central Club Contacts

Club profiles automatically display latest social media posts and disregards needs of manual updates. Format would also be designed in a more visually pleasing way.

User Experience

Better user experience for both members and officers. CPP students have an easy-to-use interface to discover any club and organization on campus. Club officers can promote and automate member registration, calendar events, and social media postings.
