Our Project: CPP Connect

Problem

Many CPP students struggle to make friends and build connections, especially newcomers and exchange students. Existing social apps are too broad and not focused on CPP community bonding.

Solution

CPP Connection is a web platform exclusively for Cal Poly Pomona students to meet, match for friendship, and build lasting campus connections.

Key Features

  • Random pairing system for friendship matching
  • Filtering by interests, major, and background
  • CPP email verification
  • Built-in messaging system
  • Profile creation and management
