About SeePhysics

Simulations Made by Students for Students

We're four computer science students currently attending Cal Poly Pomona. As part of our Software Engineering class (CS 4800) we had to do a semester long project. We all agreed we found a few concepts from our physics lectures challenging. So we thought up a solution which was to implement interactive problems and SeePhysics was born.

  • Gravitational Forces
  • Motions & Kinematics

Our Services

Expert Coders

The simulations were all created from scratch by our team with 15+ years of coding experience.

Practical Designs

Designs made with the idea of presenting concepts in simple formats with clear information and simplicity.

Powered by Matter.js

Matter.js is a 2D physics engine for the web created by Liam Brummitt @liabru.

Manipulate Variables

Change how an object interacts within the simulation to see how different values affect it like it would in the real world.

Student Creation

As students we have recent experience of taking a class in physics and wishing we had extra tools available for us.

24/7 Support

Including support to create new simulations for specific professors and answer questions from users.
