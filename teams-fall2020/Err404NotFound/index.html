Project

SuperiorDPR

Cal Poly Pomona's current Degree Progress Report (DPR) is an inefficient and confusing tool that computer science students use to determine which classes they need to graduate. Err404NotFound wanted to create a superior DPR for Cal Poly Pomona computer science students to easily determine what classes they need to complete to graduate.
