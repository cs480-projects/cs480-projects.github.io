Our Goal

Our goal is to create an application that allows a work schedule to be generated that fits around your schedule.

Project Specifics

We are making a calender to be used by both employees and employers to generate a work schedule for student workers that does not conflict with their school schedules. The schedule will be generated and displayed on a weekly calender.
